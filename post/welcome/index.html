Basil Growing

Recently, I have gotten into gardening, mainly out of a desire for fresh herbs (which are not cheap). My girlfriend’s mom gave me some spare Basil seedlings (THANK YOU!!) and after transplanting them into a small pot, I took them home. The transplant had a rather devastating effect on them and they were a bit down on their luck. I decided to give them a splash of water and record a timelapse of them perking up. Here is the code that I hacked together quickly in Python to do that:

from cv2 import *
import time

cam = VideoCapture(0)

for i in range(0,300):
    s, img = cam.read()
    if s:
        imwrite("pics/basil{}.jpg".format(i),img)
       
    time.sleep(60)

It should be noted that this is almost straight copy pasta from a stack overflow question. After that spit out 300 pictures of my basil bois, I just had to assemble them into a video. I used OpenShot and ffmpeg to do this, OpenShot to (very poorly) add all of the images and create a video, and then ffmpeg to reverse that video because OpenShot is exceptionally bad at doing things. Here is the final result!!
